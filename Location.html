Our Branches

From a humble start at Kenyatta Road in 2015, Metal Masta Artistry has grown into a network of workshops serving communities across Kenya.

Ruiru Branch

Located at the heart of Ruiru, serving both residential and industrial clients.

Juja Branch

Our Juja workshop specializes in creative designs and institutional projects.

Githurai Branch

Trusted by local businesses and households in the bustling Githurai area.

Kasarani Branch

Our Kasarani team provides fast and reliable service for urban projects.

Keno - Murang’a Branch

Extending our reach to Murang’a, serving both rural and urban communities.
