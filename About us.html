About Metal Masta Artistry

Crafting Strength. Forging Dreams. Building the Future.

Our Journey

Founded in 2015 along Kenyatta Road, Metal Masta Artistry began as a small workshop with a big vision — to transform raw metal into enduring artistry. What started as a humble space has grown into a trusted name in the metal works industry.

Over the years, our commitment to quality, precision, and innovation has seen us expand to multiple branches across Kenya, including Ruiru, Juja, Githurai, Kasarani, and Keno-Murang’a.

From serving families and small businesses to partnering with government projects and institutions, our work stands as a testament to dedication and craftsmanship.

Our Mission & Values

At Metal Masta Artistry, we believe that every weld, cut, and polish is a story in the making. Our mission is to empower communities through durable, innovative, and artistic metal solutions.
